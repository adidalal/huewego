What we're Cooking

Start out with a large batch of Philip Hue. Mix in a good helping of the Pebble smart watch. Finish with a cleverly designed Android app.

The result? A wildly addicting, multiplayer experience that is sure to delight people of all ages by offering a chance to relieve their youth.

How to Play

Players start at one end of a field or long room, and wait for their signal to go, the green light. As long as the light stays green, they run as far as they can, until the light turns red again. Anyone that continues moving will automatically lose, thanks to the accelerometer support built into the pebble. All of this is controlled by the Curator, who has the power to set the signals in the palm of their hand.
